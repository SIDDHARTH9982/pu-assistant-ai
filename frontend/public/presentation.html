Project Presentation

PU Assistant AI

An intelligent, 24/7 campus companion built specifically for Poornima University using modern web technologies and Generative AI.

Made by Siddharth

↓
The Need

Problem Statement

Current challenges faced by students and university administration.

🕒

Information Delay

Students and parents struggle to find specific fees, hostel, or faculty details quickly outside of office hours.

🧩

Fragmented Data

University information is scattered across multiple pages, PDFs, and notice boards, making it hard to search.

The Innovation

Our Solution

A centralized AI Brain for the university using RAG Architecture.

🧠

Targeted AI

Unlike standard ChatGPT, this AI operates on a curated database of Poornima University facts, ensuring 100% relevant answers.

⚡

Instant & 24/7

Users get immediate, conversational answers on admissions, courses, and facilities at any time of day.

🛡️

Admin Control

A secure admin dashboard allows staff to update the AI's knowledge base and post live notices instantly without coding.

Engineering

Technology Stack

Built globally on an industrial-grade modern full-stack framework.

⚛️ React.js (Frontend)
🟢 Node.js (Backend)
🍃 MongoDB (Database)
🧠 Google Gemini Pro (AI)
🎨 Tailwind CSS (Styling)
🔐 JWT Auth (Security)
